Facilities

The City of Camarillo has provided a bike bath that runs along Callegus Creek.  Between the bike path and the San Simeon community is what is called the LinearPark.  The LinearPark is the landscaped portion, including benches, trash cans and doggie bag dispensers, which are the property of San Simeon and the cost of the maintenance is paid for by San Simeon.   The Association and Board of Directors continuously works on keeping this common area a beautiful and pleasurable part of our community.  Please be respectful and keep all people and animals out of the landscaping and be sure to pick up all of  your dogs waste.

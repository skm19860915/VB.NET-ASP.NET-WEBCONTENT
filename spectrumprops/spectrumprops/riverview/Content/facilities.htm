Facilities
Riverview Ventura HOA is in a great location in Ventura.  It has a beautiful night view of the city.  Riverview also has beautiful landscaping throughout the community.

  

          
Residents are able to enjoy the swimming Pools & Spas within the community.  there are 3 Pools & 3 Spas, 1 for each phase.  Spas are heated year round.  The Pools are heated beginning May 1st of each year. 

  

  
Residents are also able to play tennis at the community's exclusive tennis court.

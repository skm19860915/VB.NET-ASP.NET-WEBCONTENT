 As a show of appreciation for the efforts of the firefighters to protect resident’s dwellings in Westlake Pointe from the ravages of the Woolsey Fire, lunches were served to sixteen firefighters at Fire Station # 144 in Westlake Village on January 22, 23, and 24th. ‘Brent’s Deli’ supplied the food to the Westlake Pointe HOA.  A ’thank you card’ was presented to each of the three shifts at the fire station and to ‘Brent's Deli'. Genuine appreciation was expressed by the firefighters for this recognition. 

The names of the personnel at the FS 144 are as follows:

'A’ Shift  - Captain Pat Wilson, Engineer Dan Hayashi, Firefighter Phil Najemnck, 

‘B’ Shift - Captain Jay Fernandez, Firefighter Jon Galiher, Firefighter Matt Cristea, Firefighter Glen Stratton

                   Firefighter  Jeff Berry

‘C’ Shift - Captain Derek Bart, Engineer Rich Ward, Firefighter Anthony Duncan, Firefighter AJ Rodriguez

Note -  four visiting firefighters were served on the above dates
